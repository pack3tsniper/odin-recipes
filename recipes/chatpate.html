Ingredients

  • Puff Rice
  • Dalmot
  • Oil
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Chickpeas
  • Lemon
  • Potato
  • Peas
  • Chat masala
  • Salt
  • Chilli
  • Wai Wai
  • Chili powder
  1. Add the tomato, onion, green chili, cilantro, lemon juice, green peas, chickpeas, potato, all seasoning (chaat masala, chili powder, lemon juice, salt to taste), and wai wai seasonings to a mixing bowl.
  2. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.
  3. Crush the Wai Wai noodles into smaller pieces. Then, add noodles, puff rice, dalmot, and oil to the bowl. Mix well until all the ingredients are combined.
  4. Enjoy the delicious Wai Wai Chatpate. Serve immediately to retain its crunchiness; otherwise, it will be soggy.
